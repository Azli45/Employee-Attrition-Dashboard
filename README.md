# Employee Attrition Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to visualize and analyze employee attrition patterns, developed as part of the [Employee Attrition Intelligence System](https://github.com/Azli45/Employee-Attrition-Intelligence-System) project.

## Overview

This dashboard transforms HR analytics data into clear, actionable visuals, helping identify who is leaving, why, and where attrition risk is highest across the organization. It complements the predictive model from the main project, which achieved 88% accuracy and a 0.84 F1-score, by giving stakeholders a visual, drill down view of the underlying trends.

## Business Problems Addressed

This dashboard was built to help answer the following key business questions:

1. Understanding and Reducing Attrition: identifying high turnover roles, teams, and locations to support business continuity.
2. Targeted Management Strategies: enabling focused retention strategies for roles, departments, and demographics with recurring turnover.
3. High Attrition Rates in Specific Departments: pinpointing which departments face the highest attrition to prioritize retention efforts.
4. Job Role Influence on Attrition: identifying job roles that significantly influence attrition to address role specific retention challenges.
5. Distance from Home as a Factor: analyzing whether longer commutes correlate with higher attrition.
6. Work Culture and Compensation: highlighting the link between overtime, compensation, and employee morale to support retention.
7. Employee Satisfaction Ratings: assessing job satisfaction levels and their impact on attrition.
8. Analyzing Turnover Trends: tracking attrition trends over time to support data driven decision making.


## Tools and Tech Used

* Power BI Desktop: dashboard design and DAX calculations
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): custom measures and KPIs
* Power Query: data cleaning and transformation
